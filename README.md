🌀 PART 2: THE SEPARATION
(Center-Left)
The high-pressure mixture enters the Cyclonic Separator.
Like a high-speed spin cycle, it flings the mud, rocks, and liquid water to the bottom.
☁️ Pure, clean Dry Steam shoots out the top.

Hot Brine + Steam
         ↓
[Cyclonic Separator]
      ↙            ↘
  Dry Steam      Hot Liquid Brine

  ⚡ PART 3: THE POWER GENERATION
(Center)
The Dry Steam flows directly into the Containerized Turbine/Generator Module.
The steam spins the turbine blades at massive RPMs, which turns the generator shaft.
This is where the kinetic energy is converted into:
⚡ 1 MEGAWATT OF CLEAN, BASELOAD POWER
Ready to be connected to your digital load.

Dry Steam
     ↓
[Turbine / Generator]
     ↓
⚡ 1 MW Power Output

❄️ PART 4: THE SAFE RETURN
(Right Side)
The spent steam, now at a lower pressure, enters the Dry Condenser Array.
💨 Large fans blow air over the pipes, cooling the steam back into liquid water.

Spent Steam
      ↓
[Dry Condenser]
      ↓
💧 Cooled Water

♻️ PART 5: THE RE-INJECTION
(Bottom Right)
The High-Pressure Injection Pump forces the cooled water back down Hole 2 (Injection Well).
This keeps the underground reservoir stable and helps prevent contamination of surrounding groundwater.

💧 Water
      ↓
[Injection Pump]
      ↓
[Injection Well]
      ↓
🌋 Geothermal Reservoir
